Colors!

Background colors for individual elements

Use style="background-color:color*" in the tag to specify the background color that you want.

Custom Background Colors

To make custome cullors use rgb(r, b, g), rgba(r, g, b, a), hsl(h, s, l), or hsla(h, s, l, a) to make custom colors.

You can also use hex values like #ff00ee where in order for left to right the values represent rrggbb. Since the scale is from 0-255 and the largest number possible with 2 hexidecimal digits is 255 you have the full range in two characters.
