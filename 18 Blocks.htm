Blocks <div>

The <div> makes a block. a block is similar to the body section of html. Anything can go inside of it. It is its own section of the page. You can have blocks within blocks.

Spans <span>

The <span> is used to group text. Suppose you wanted to have every word in a sentence be a different color while remaining in the same paragraph. Then you would use spans.
