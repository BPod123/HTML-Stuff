Blocks/Divisions <div>

A block/division <div> is a section of the page. Similar to how the <body> is one large block, a division can is like a <body> within a <body>.
Here we will have a block with a yellow background inside of a block with a white background.

            <div style="background-color:white; color:black;">
                <h3 This is a block with a white background.</h3>
                <hr>
                <p> I acts like a <body> would. </p>

                <div style="background-color:yellow;">
                    <h3 This is a block with a yellow background.</h3>
                    <hr>
                    <p> It acts like a <body> would. </p>
                </div>
                <h3> This is in the white-background block again </h3>
            </div>
