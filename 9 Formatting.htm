Bold vs Strong and
Italicized vs Emphasized

strong and emphasized text are "important" text whereas bold and italic text
is just bolded and italicized.

Use <mark> to highlight text.
Use <del> to put a slash through text.
Use <ins> to underline text.
Use <sub> to turn text into subscript.
